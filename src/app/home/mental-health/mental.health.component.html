Mental Health Survey for Covid-19

Since December 2019, there has been an outbreak of pneumonia of an unknown etiology that was first reported in Wuhan, Hubei Province, China. Following the outbreak, a novel corona virus, SARS-CoV-2, was identified as the causative virus for the pandemic in China and other parts of the world.
Over time, the Covid 19 pandemic has become a part of our life. This changes our lifestyle and may also affect our behavior and mental health. People have experienced different kinds of problems and unexpected situations because of Covid.
The purpose of this survey is to find out the effect of Covid 19 on the participants personal life. Based on some basic questions and answering them with their own experience we try to illustrate the change to their life because of Covid 19.
Among the participants 90% is student and they participate in the survey without any kind of personal data. 100% participants are between the age of 21 to 40.

About 30% of them were affected by the covid 19.Pandmic also increase the relaxing and exercise time for participants.

During the pandemic, people were worried about the situation, and our survey shows, how much participants were concerned about the world situation of Covid situation. Around 36% people were chacking daily news to undersent the severity of the disease.

The mental pressure which is caused by covid is unexplainable and unmeasurable. With some point, we try to find out our participant's experience on their phycological behavior. It shows, the percentage of depression is highest wile anxiety and loneliness is also having high percentage.

The survey also show that the user paid more attention to their mental health, and they did different activity to keep them more enthusiastic. The increment of participants concern on their mental health is clear in this portion of the survey.

In a situation like the pandemic covid 19, support from family and friends is very important to survive the time with less depression or anxiety. In the survey we asked our participant, how was their experience? About 30% strongly agreed that they get support from friends and family while about 46% is agreed on that term.Covid also change the behavior of daily life. 100% agreed on the term.
